Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3⁄4cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspooms yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For The Filling:

  • ⅔ cup darl brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs,
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined
  4. knead the dough into alarge bowl and cover it with plastic wrap
  5. leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
  6. roll out dough into a large rectangle
  7. mix fillings ingredients; spread on dough
  8. roll dough into swiss roll shape
  9. cut into 3cm sections; place in baking tray
  10. bake at 350F/180C for 20-25Mins.
